Websites and Soundscapes:

Chess:

As mentioned on my Bio page (link to it here), one of my favorite pastimes is the game of chess. In a previous CSE 125R course that I took for public speaking in the online domain, I had the opportunity to create a whole site about the game (created with WordPress and Elementor). Check it out here: chessforall.space.

Music, the Future, and Accessibility:

  • In a previous writing course I took known as CAT3, we were tasked as a group of four to examine the intersection of music and future technology. Our group emphasized this intersection by music creators who are disabled or otherwise more physically limited, and created a site about it here: Music and Tech.
    (Also note that credits are provided on that site for the soundscape.)
  • Also available on this linked site is a soundscape that I and one other member co-created from other music clips, emphasizing those created by the physically disabled, and those who used novel technologies to create their sounds.
